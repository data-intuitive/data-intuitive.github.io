What we can do for you

Bioinformatics

Our team of experienced bioinformaticians can help you analyze and interpret large, complex datasets, such as those generated by high-throughput sequencing experiments. We can help you identify patterns, trends, and relationships in your data, and develop custom algorithms and software tools to help you make sense of it all.

Data science

Our data scientists can help you develop custom data-driven solutions for your business or research project. We can help you design and implement data pipelines, build predictive models, and perform data visualization and reporting.

Pipeline development

We can help you build custom data pipelines to automate the flow of data from various sources, through various processing and analysis steps, and into your final data products. We have expertise in a range of pipeline development technologies, including Viash, Docker, Nextflow, and Apache Spark.

Training and consultation

In addition to our core services in bioinformatics and data science, we also offer a range of support services, including training and consultation. We are committed to helping our clients build in-house capabilities and expertise in data analysis and interpretation.
